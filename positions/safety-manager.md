**About the Position:**

Safety and Compliance Officer will report to Safety Director. This role will be responsible for assisting Safety Director in day to day functions of jobsite safety management including but not limited to training and orientation of field personnel, traffic control, accident report forms, MSHA, OSHA and Pipeline Safety Bureau training, reporting and interface, calling 311 Line Spots, etc. The Safety and Compliance Officer will work side by side with the Safety Director, Project Managers, Foremen and Division Superintendents on a daily basis to assist in and carry out the responsibilities of the safety department and must have the capability to manually complete any activity.
